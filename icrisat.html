ICRISAT Dataset Information

ICRISAT dataset provides comprehensive and historical agricultural data across various districts. It includes metrics on areas, productions and yields of multiple crops, facilitating analysis of crop patterns and productivity.

Dataset Structure

Shape of ICRISAT Data set: (16146, 80) i.e., it contains 80 attributes and 16146 rows/items
